Introduction

For many websites accessibility was not a consideration when they were first created. Thus there are likely to be accessibility barriers that make it difficult or impossible for some people with disabilities to use the site. Addressing accessibility can be tackled as a standalone project or part of broader site maintenance activities. In either case there are approaches that help make the process more efficient and effective.

What is this document for?

This document provides you with information on what is important when seeking to improve the accessibility of a website. The related document on Implementation Planning may be useful as many of the topics discussed are relevant to improving accessibility for existing websites.

Who is it for?

This document is for project managers, project team leaders, website owners and managers, web developers, and small business owners. It is likely that you will be relatively new to accessibility.

Using this document

The document presents the information as a structured list of topics that you will need to consider. Information from prior topics are often required for subsequent activities so you may need to consider how you plan to approach them.

Determine Scope and Target

By specifying what websites or pages are to be assessed and to what standard you will be able to confine the scope to something that is achievable.

Key actions

  • Set a standard

    Most organizations use WCAG as a target for accessibility and specify what level of success criteria they would aim to meet. You may already have an organizational policy that specifies what standard and level you need to achieve.

  • Define what is to be evaluated

    It is important to clearly define the extent of the pages that are to be assessed and improved. This may be a single website or a part of a larger site. You will need to carefully consider your timescales and available resources when determining the scope. Where you initially have limited resources focusing on key user journeys and pages may bring the most significant benefits.
